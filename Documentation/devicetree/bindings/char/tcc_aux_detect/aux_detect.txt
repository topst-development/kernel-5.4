Telechips aux_detect device

Required property:
- compatible:
		telechpis,aux_detect

- pinctrl-0:
		<&aux_detect_pin>

- aux-gpios:
		aux detect gpio

Example:
		aux_detect {
			compatible = "telechips,aux_detect";
			status = "okay";

			pinctrl-names = "default";
			pinctrl-0 = <&aux_detect_pin>;

			aux-gpios = <&gpmb 11 1>;
			aux-active = <1>;
		};

Telechips Touch Sharing Handler

Required properties:
- compatible:
    telechips,tcc_touch_bridge

- mbox-names:
	tb-mbox

- mboxes:
	<&cb_mbox TCC_CB_MBOX_TCHSHARE>

- mbox-id:
	TCC_MBOX_TCHSHARE_ID

Example:
	tstb:tcc_touch_bridge {
		compatible = "telechips,tcc_touch_bridge";
		mbox-names ="tb-mbox";
		mboxes = <&cb_mbox TCC_CB_MBOX_TCHSHARE>;
		mbox-id = TCC_MBOX_TCHSHARE_ID;
	};
};
